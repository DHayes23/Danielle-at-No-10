Our Favourite Night Time Cleanse

A healthy night time routine is essential to keep you looking and feeling your best. After a long, hard and sometimes stressful day, it can be difficult to unwind and find that energy to cleanse and prepare your skin for bed. But fear not! Our nightly routine only takes two minutes leaving you with plenty of time to catch those valuable Zs.

We start with the Garnier Micellar Gel Face Wash for Sensitive Skin. Simply apply a small blob onto a finger and apply it evenly across your entire face. Ensure that you apply the gel around the eyes, albeit carefully. Do this for as little as 2-3 days and your face should feel revitalised, fresh and thoroughly clean. After the gel has dried in, a splash of cold water will help tighten pores. Pat dry with a soft towel to help with any excess water.

For the next step we suggest using two drops of lavender oil mixed with two drops of argan oil to prepare a solution that will work while you sleep. (Mix and warm in the palm our your hand) Once the mixture is warm, gently apply it to your face. One handy upside to this step is that it can be done from the comfort of your own bed!

An extra little treat, especially for those with dry skin is to use an essential oil defusor with lavender. This has been shown to increase the benefits of a good night's sleep.

We would love to hear about your nightly routine! Feel free to share your relaxation tips on our Twitter page using #MyNight.
