How we're combatting Covid-19 at No. 10

At Danielle's at No. 10 our foremost priority is the safety of our clients and staff. In recognition of the danger posed by the Covid-19 pandemic, we are complying with governmental guidelines. As such, we are operating within the confines of the National Framework for Living with Covid-19. This means that our business can open only at Lockdown Level 3 or lower. At all times during your visit to us we will be using PPE (Personal Protective Equipent) including gloves, face masks and face shields. Our clients are required to wear a face mask at all times, except at those times that the wearing of a mask would impede our ability to provide your chosen treatment. We have committed to even more regular and thorough cleanings of all of our surfaces and equipment. Thank you for your cooperation at this difficult time - Danielle

All About Us

Who We Are

We are a two-woman team of qualified beauty professionals, each with years of experience in our respective fields. I am Danielle, the owner of Danielle's at No. 10 and have been practicing in the beauty industry for 10 years. I specialise in nail care, advanced skin care and hair removal techniques. My colleague, Ciara, has been a practicing beauty therapist for 8 years, and specialises in tanning, massage therapy and make up application. Together we aim to provide you with an industry leading service.

What We Do

We offer an extensive list of treatments to have you looking and feeling fabulous from head to toe.
