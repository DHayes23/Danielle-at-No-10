All About Us

Who We Are

We are a two-woman team of qualified beauty professionals, each with years of experience in our respective fields. I am Danielle, the owner of Danielle's at No. 10 and have been practicing in the beauty industry for 10 years. I specialise in nail care, advanced skin care and hair removal techniques. My colleague, Ciara, has been a practicing beauty therapist for 8 years, and specialises in tanning, massage therapy and make up application. Together we aim to provide you with an industry leading service.

What We Do

We offer an extensive list of treatments to have you looking and feeling fabulous from head to toe. From essentials such as waxing and tanning to our luxurious massages and Guinot facials, you can count on us to make you feel prepared and pampered. Take a look at our Treatments page for a full list of what we offer. We regularly expand our skillsets and the list of services we offer is always growing. We love innovation at No. 10 and so we are always on the lookout for new and exciting treatments to include in our range.
