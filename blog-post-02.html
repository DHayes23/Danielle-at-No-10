Caring For Your Lashes

We style our lashes almost every day so that we can look our best, but this constant strain can lead to our lashes becoming damaged. When our lashes are weakend the hair fibres start to break down. They don't look as good, they become more likely to clump and they even fall out. For these reasons it's important to maintain your lashes in the same way as the rest of your body. To that end, here's our mini-guide on how to show your lashes some love.

Ensure that you are using a high quality mascara. Eyelashes are delicate, and only the best mascaras treat them with the respect they deserve. As difficult as it can be to find the motivation sometimes, it's essential that you thoroughly remove your mascara every night before bed. Even with a high quality mascara, having your lashes under the effects of chemicals for long periods such as while you sleep can have a negative impact on your lash density. Let your lashes breathe every night and you'll have a set that inspires envy!

Take your lash care that step further by adding in a cleansing routine. Use a cream cleanser such as Facetheory's Vitamin C Face Cleanser and Makup Remover. Creams like this one allow you to go the extra mile in making sure that your face is fully makeup free by day's end.
