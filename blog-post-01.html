Our Seasonal Handcare Routine

Our hands go through a lot and so it's important to spend time caring for them. It's especially important to adapt your hand care routine to the changing seasons. We have found that the following tips help to keep our hands in tip-top shape!

During the winter months, cold weather often causing skin to crack and lose its natural moisture. Cracking damages the skin and makes it feel rougher to the touch and can cause blotchy red patches. That's why in cold weather we recommend using a high quality moisturiser twice a day. We use Neutrogena to combat the moisture sucking winter cold. Whenever possible, we wear gloves while outside in the low temperatures. We have to interact with so many things in the world using our hands every day. A nice pair of gloves can compliment your winter look while also keeping protecting your hands from extra abrasive icy surfaces and the drying air. If your hands have already fallen foul of the frost, don't despair, we have a great remedy. Eucerin Advanced Repair Hand Cream can heal the damage done by the cold. Simply apply it twice a day, once in the morning and once before bed, and within a week your hands will have returned to their full glory.

For those with extra sensitive skin, the summer months and hot temperatures can be equally harmful to your hands. Often the heat of the sun beating down on your hands can cause irritation, again leading to red blotchy marks and an annoying itch. First, we recommend continuing to use a high quality moisturiser twice a day, and if you have sensitive skin Aveeno is the cream for you. Developed with a more natural formula, Aveeno is unlikely to aggravate otherwise grumpy skin. More importantly for those with sensitive skin however is the use of a high SPF sunscreen to protect the hands. It's easy to forget to apply sunscreen to the very things you're using to apply the sunscreen with, however our hands are usually exposed for long periods of the day.

We hope these tips will help you to keep your hands in top condition year round!
